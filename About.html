My Design Philosophy

  • Business-and-User Center:
    Balancing business and user needs is challenging. However, without business and marketing consideration, user-centered approach can not always make sense.
  • Beauty Makes Difference:
    The visible beauty is a reflection of the beauty behind the appearance. It is about my design attitude and design competence.
  • Design Behaviors instead of Things:
    Design is not merely making things, but making things to use. How people behave with the thing is worth more thinking.
  • Design for Smart Life:
    Design connects online world and real world seamlessly. To create meaningful product for smart life, I always think about experiences in both worlds simultaneously.
  • Be a Better Person First:
    Design is about how you get along with the world, the life and other people. My work should be the expression of my personalities.
